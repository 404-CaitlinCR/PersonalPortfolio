    Volunteer Work:

    Lego NXT robots:

    During my last year of high school, my Computer Science teacher gave myself and the other girls in her class to organize and even to help inspire other girls in grades 7-8 to join STEM as well as get hands on experience with Lego NXT robots.

    Our goal was to teach them about the important women in STEM and their contributions to the field, the areas it can applied and its benefits. After a short presentation we split the girls into smaller teams who would work together to build robots and the create code for them to help it complete specific challenges.
